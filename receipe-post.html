Sausages with sauerkraut and potatoes

Prep: 1hr & 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 4 Servings

01.

Boil the potatoes in the salt in salted water. Be careful not to overcook.

02.

Cut the onion into strips and sauté it in a little lard until golden.

03.

Add boiled, peeled and chopped potatoes.

04.

Pepper well and add the red smoked paprika and combine everything.

05.

If necessary, cover with a little water and simmer for another five minutes.

06.

Drizzle the sausages with beer and fry them briefly in a pan.

07.

Remove the sausages from the pan and add the sauerkraut, a little mustard, apple and red pepper to the fat.

08.

Simmer for about 20 minutes.

09.

Put roasted potatoes, cabbage and sausage in a bowl.

10.

If desired, grate the aromatic melted cheese and bake for 15 minutes in an oven preheated to 200 Celsius.
